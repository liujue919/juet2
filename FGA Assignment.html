FGA Assignment

1. What are 5 things needed on a resume?

Reply: You should always include the following five sections on your resume:

a. Contact details

b. Resume introduction

c. Education

d. Experience

e. Skills

2. Why is it a good thing to carry extra resumes to your job interview?

Reply: By bringing extra copies, you immediately show interviewers you're a person who prepares for their responsibilities. Often, you and the interviewer may review specific places on your resume, such as the skills and professional experience section throughout the discussion.

3. Is it possible to save a file as a HTML Document?

Reply: Yes. Follow these steps to save your resume in HTML format: Create your resume in a word processor. Select "File," "Save As" and "Text Document" or "Text Only" from the format drop-down. With your resume in text format, insert the formatting tags that convert it to HTML. Open your text resume with an editor.

4. Can you use any font designs and font size when writing a resume?

Reply: a. Acceptable resume fonts include Calibri, Cambria, Garamond, Helvetica, Georgia, Tahoma, Verdana, Trebuchet MS, and Book Antiqua.

b. The ideal resume font size is between 10 and 12 pt.

5. What are Five things that you should not put on a resume?

Reply: a. Too much information

b. Loads of hobbies and interests

c. Irrelevant social media accounts

d. Spelling or grammatical errors

e.  Previous salary details

6. Should you add a picture when submitting your resume? Why or Why not?

Reply: In general, avoid putting a photo on your resume. Studies show recruiters spend less than seven seconds looking at a resume, so you don’t want to distract them in any way. Resume photos can be a distraction. If you work in real estate or are a performer in the entertainment industry, it is appropriate to have a photo on your resume.

7. Should you print your resume on bright colored paper.  Explain?

Reply: none. It is perfectly acceptable to print your resume on a regular white piece of paper from home with your printer. With technology as it is today, the employer has no expectations that you will hand in a heavy stock, gold colored resume.

8. Why are resumes important?

Reply: I can present ourselves to employers through our resume. A resume is the first impression me make on an employer. Employers know me through resumes. So this is very important.

9. Is okay to tell lies on resume? Why or why not?

Reply: I think lying is bad. Lying on my resume will turn me off to employers. I will never be hired by this company in the future. The most important thing is that lies will eventually be exposed. Once interviewed, it will be revealed. So it's better not to lie.

10. What is meant by the objective on a resume?

Reply: A resume objective can be an excellent tool to communicate your career goals to potential employers. Many professionals use resume objectives to highlight their skills and share how they can provide value to a company.
